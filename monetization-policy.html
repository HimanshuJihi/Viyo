Monetization Policy

At Viyou, we believe in rewarding our creators for the engaging content they bring to the platform. Our Creator Monetization Program allows eligible users to earn money based on the performance of their content.

1. Eligibility Criteria

To apply for monetization, creators must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • A minimum of 200 Followers on your Viyou profile.
  • A minimum of 10,000 Total Video Views across your uploaded content.
  • Consistent compliance with Viyou's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

2. How Earnings Are Calculated (Revenue Tiers)

Once approved, your content will generate revenue based on an advanced Tier system:

  • Pre-Roll Video Ads: You earn revenue based on the impressions generated by pre-roll ads running on your long videos.
  • Dynamic Revenue Share: Your share of the revenue increases as your audience grows:
    • Tier 1 (50% Share): 200+ Followers and 10,000+ Views
    • Tier 2 (55% Share): 1,000+ Followers and 100,000+ Views
    • Tier 3 (60% Share): 50,000+ Followers and 1M+ Views
    • Tier 4 (65% Share): 100,000+ Followers and 5M+ Views
    • Tier 5 (70% Share): 1M+ Followers and 10M+ Views

3. Payouts & Withdrawals

You can track your estimated earnings directly from the Creator Studio dashboard.

  • The minimum withdrawal threshold is ₹500.
  • Withdrawal requests can be made via UPI ID or direct Bank Transfer.
  • Payments are typically processed within 3-5 business days after approval.

4. Invalid Traffic & Anti-Fraud Policy

Viyou strictly monitors traffic to protect our advertisers and ensure fair payouts.

  • Using bots, automated scripts, or artificial methods to inflate views or clicks is strictly prohibited.
  • Clicking on your own ads or encouraging others to do so will result in an immediate ban and forfeiture of earnings.
  • We enforce a strict rate limit on ad clicks to prevent click fraud.

5. Content Guidelines

Monetized content must be original and adhere to our policies. Content containing abusive language, explicit material, or copyrighted material without permission will be demonetized, deleted, and may lead to account suspension.

2. कमाई की गणना कैसे की जाती है (Revenue Tiers)

एक बार स्वीकृत होने के बाद, आपका कंटेंट निम्नलिखित टियर सिस्टम (Tier System) के आधार पर रेवेन्यू उत्पन्न करना शुरू कर देगा:

  • आप अपने लॉन्ग वीडियोस पर चलने वाले प्री-रोल विज्ञापनों (Pre-Roll Ads) के माध्यम से कमाई करते हैं।
  • आपका रेवेन्यू शेयर (Revenue Share) आपके टियर (स्तर) पर निर्भर करता है:
    • Tier 1 (50% शेयर): 200+ फ़ॉलोअर्स और 10,000+ व्यूज
    • Tier 2 (55% शेयर): 1,000+ फ़ॉलोअर्स और 100,000+ व्यूज
    • Tier 3 (60% शेयर): 50,000+ फ़ॉलोअर्स और 1M+ व्यूज
    • Tier 4 (65% शेयर): 100,000+ फ़ॉलोअर्स और 5M+ व्यूज
    • Tier 5 (70% शेयर): 1M+ फ़ॉलोअर्स और 10M+ व्यूज

3. भुगतान और निकासी (Payouts)

हम अपने विज्ञापनदाताओं की सुरक्षा और उचित भुगतान सुनिश्चित करने के लिए ट्रैफ़िक की सख्ती से निगरानी करते हैं।

  • अपने स्वयं के विज्ञापनों पर जानबूझकर क्लिक करने से तुरंत प्रतिबंध (Ban) लग सकता है और कमाई जब्त की जा सकती है।
